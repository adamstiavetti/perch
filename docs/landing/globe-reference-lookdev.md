# Globe Reference Lookdev

Status: active globe-only reference target.

## Target Traits

- Oceans are deep navy/blue with visible tonal variation, not pure black.
- The front face has cinematic blue fill, so the globe never reads as a black void.
- Surface detail is visible through cloud, ocean, terrain, and shadow breakup.
- Continents and coastlines are readable but not chalky, pale, or pasted on.
- City lights are warm amber/gold and embedded into real geography.
- Dense light regions should glow subtly without becoming random dots.
- Atmosphere is a thin blue rim, strongest at the edge and top glow.
- Atmosphere must not coat the front face or make the globe look like glass.
- Ice and snow are visible but controlled; no harsh white blobs.
- The globe must feel dimensional, premium, and aviation-cinematic.

## Negative Checks

- Not a raw NASA texture.
- Not a blue glass ball.
- Not a black sphere plus city lights.
- Not a flat map projection.
- Not a bright daytime Earth.
- Not cyan haze or milky atmosphere.

Generate larger controlled variants that target the supplied Deadhead globe reference directly:

- Reference Fill Match: mid-face navy/blue richness.
- Surface Detail Match: richer surface/cloud/terrain breakup.
- City/Atmosphere Match: warmer embedded city lights and thin rim energy.
- Combined Reference Push: best combined candidate for later hero reinsertion.
