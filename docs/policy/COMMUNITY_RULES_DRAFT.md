# Community Rules Draft

Status: draft user-facing copy for founder/legal review

Last updated: 2026-06-14

jmpseat is for verified aviation workers sharing practical, safe utility
knowledge. The product principle is utility first, community second, social feed
last.

This draft is not final legal policy. Founder/legal review is required before
broader launch or broader private-beta use.

## The Short Version

Help people. Keep it practical. Protect privacy. Do not post information that
could expose passengers, crew, employers, security procedures, exact crew
hotels, live operations, or confidential company material.

## What Belongs

Good jmpseat posts and questions include:

- DFW commuting and parking questions
- terminal and ground logistics reminders at a high level
- food, coffee, break, and local tips without sensitive tie-ins
- new-to-DFW questions
- layover planning ideas that avoid exact crew hotel exposure
- practical answers from verified workers
- respectful corrections and updates
- reports of content that creates safety, privacy, spam, or harassment risk

## What Does Not Belong

Do not post, request, or hint at:

- passenger names, photos, itineraries, medical details, seat/trip identifiers,
  incidents, contact details, or other passenger private information
- exact crew hotel names, addresses, rosters, routes, date patterns, or details
  that expose where crew are staying
- live crew location, public nearby crew tracking, or real-time crew movement
- airport/security procedures, restricted access details, screening workarounds,
  badge/security processes, or safety-rule evasion
- live operations-sensitive information, staffing details, live irregular-ops
  information, or details that could affect safety or operations
- confidential employer documents, manuals, portal screenshots, internal memos,
  non-public policies, or protected systems content
- airline portal credentials, schedule scraping, roster/calendar integrations,
  flight loads, or non-rev load infrastructure
- harassment, threats, doxxing, hate, retaliation, or sexualized targeting
- impersonation of workers, unions, airlines, airports, employers, jmpseat, or
  moderators
- spam, scams, vendor self-promotion, phishing, malware, scraping, or abusive
  automation
- dating/swiping, unsafe meetup pressure, or public crew-tracking behavior
- marketplace payments or transaction/dispute behavior

## Anonymous Publicly, Accountable Internally

jmpseat may show a public handle or safe member label. That does not mean
activity is anonymous to jmpseat.

jmpseat can connect posts, reports, verification status, and moderation records
to the underlying account when needed for policy enforcement, safety, security,
support, or legal reasons.

## Use Official Sources

jmpseat is not an official operational source.

Use official/employer sources for live operations, security procedures, current
policies, duty/rest timing, safety-critical decisions, schedules, and company
requirements.

## Reporting

Report content that appears unsafe, private, harassing, spammy, off-topic, or
otherwise against these rules.

Keep report details short and focused. Do not add extra private information to a
report.

Reporter identity is not public. Public report counts are not shown in the
current product.

## Moderation

jmpseat may hide, remove, restrict, preserve, or escalate content when needed to
protect privacy, safety, security, community quality, or the private beta.

Moderation consequences may include:

- content hidden or removed
- request for clarification or correction
- warning
- temporary restriction
- access removal or suspension after human review
- escalation for safety, security, privacy, or legal review

AI does not make final bans or final moderation decisions.

## Community Tone

Be direct, useful, and kind enough that other aviation workers can actually use
the answer.

Good disagreement is allowed. Personal attacks, pile-ons, targeted harassment,
and identity exposure are not.
