# Private Beta Terms Draft

Status: draft copy for founder/legal review

Last updated: 2026-06-14

This draft is practical private-beta copy for jmpseat. It is not legal advice
and is not a final Terms of Service. Founder/legal review is required before
broader launch or broad private-beta use.

## Short Version

jmpseat is an invite-based private beta for verified aviation workers. It is
utility first, community second, and social feed last.

You may use jmpseat to ask practical questions, share safe DFW base and layover
knowledge, and report content that creates privacy, safety, or community risk.

Do not use jmpseat for passenger private information, airport/security
procedures, live operations-sensitive information, exact crew hotel exposure,
confidential company documents, harassment, threats, doxxing, impersonation,
spam, scams, public crew tracking, dating/swiping, schedule scraping, airline
portal access, or marketplace/payment activity.

## Beta Status

jmpseat is in private beta. Access is limited, invite-based, and may be changed,
paused, or removed while the product is being tested.

jmpseat may change or remove features, adjust policies, limit access, fix bugs,
or pause community activity at any time during the beta.

jmpseat does not guarantee uptime, feature availability, response time, or
continued access during the beta.

## Eligibility And Verification

jmpseat is intended for aviation workers and aviation-adjacent workers who are
eligible for the private beta.

jmpseat may require account email confirmation, beta invite access, work-email
verification, profile completion, or manual review before access to private app
surfaces.

Verification helps keep jmpseat useful and safer for aviation workers. It does
not mean jmpseat is officially verifying employment for an airline, airport,
union, or employer. It also does not create an endorsement by any airline,
airport, union, or employer.

Do not misuse another person's account, email, work email, badge, document, or
identity. Do not submit false information.

## Identity Model

jmpseat's identity principle is:

- verified privately
- anonymous publicly
- accountable internally

Your public display may use a handle or safe member label. That does not mean
your activity is anonymous to jmpseat for policy, safety, security,
verification, reporting, moderation, or legal purposes.

jmpseat may internally connect content, reports, verification state, and
moderation records to the underlying account when needed to operate the product
or enforce these terms.

## Acceptable Use

Use jmpseat for practical aviation-worker utility:

- asking DFW base questions
- sharing safe commuting, parking, terminal, food, coffee, break, and layover
  guidance
- helping verified workers find existing DFW Channels
- reporting privacy, safety, spam, harassment, or other policy issues
- giving feedback about the beta

Keep posts and reports accurate, respectful, and safe.

## Prohibited Content And Conduct

Do not post, request, encourage, or facilitate:

- harassment, threats, hate, sexualized targeting, retaliation, or unsafe
  pressure
- doxxing or private identity exposure
- impersonation of workers, employers, unions, airports, airlines, jmpseat
  staff, or moderators
- passenger names, photos, itineraries, trip identifiers, medical details,
  contact details, incidents, seat information, or other passenger private
  information
- confidential company documents, non-public manuals, portal screenshots,
  internal memos, employer-confidential policies, or protected system content
- airport/security-sensitive procedures, restricted access details, screening
  workarounds, badge/security processes, or safety-rule evasion
- live operations-sensitive information, live staffing details, live flight or
  crew movement details, live irregular-ops details, or exact real-time
  location details
- exact crew hotel exposure tied to airline, trip, route, crew, date, or
  identifiable operational pattern
- airline portal login, schedule scraping, roster/calendar integration, flight
  loads, or non-rev load infrastructure
- public nearby crew tracking, dating/swiping, or public live crew location
  behavior
- spam, scams, abusive automation, scraping, phishing, malware, or attempts to
  disrupt the service
- full marketplace payments or vendor self-service behavior
- AI-generated operational advice presented as authoritative

## User Content Responsibility

You are responsible for what you post, submit, or report.

Do not post content you do not have permission to share. Do not rely on jmpseat
as an official source for operations, safety, employer policy, legal questions,
security procedures, duty/rest timing, or current airport conditions.

Use official/employer sources for live operations, security procedures, current
policies, duty/rest timing, and safety-critical decisions.

## Moderation Rights

jmpseat may review, hide, remove, restrict, preserve, or escalate content or
activity when needed to protect users, privacy, safety, security, community
quality, legal obligations, or the beta.

Current T26E-A moderation supports DFW Channel post reporting and
operator-scoped review. Visible operator actions are narrow hide/remove actions.
Account bans, AI final moderation decisions, public moderation feeds, and public
report counts are not part of the current T26E-A foundation.

jmpseat may deny, pause, restrict, or remove account access for policy
violations, verification concerns, security concerns, abuse, or beta operations
needs.

## Reports

Use the report flow for spam, harassment, unsafe information, privacy issues,
off-topic content, or other concerns.

Reporter identity is not public. Public report counts are not shown in the
current product.

Reports may be reviewed by authorized operators. Report details should stay
focused on the issue. Do not include extra private information in report
details.

## Appeals

Private-beta appeals are draft/manual and implementation is pending.

Until a formal appeal flow is implemented, users should contact
`support@jmpseat.com` once configured with the subject prefix
`[Moderation Appeal]` for account, verification, or moderation questions.
There is no in-app appeal backend or automatic appeal workflow in the current
product.

Appeals may not restore content that exposes passengers, exact crew hotels,
airport/security-sensitive procedures, live operations-sensitive information,
doxxing, threats, or confidential documents.

## No Airline Affiliation

jmpseat is independent. jmpseat is not affiliated with, sponsored by, endorsed
by, or officially connected to any airline, airport, union, employer, or
government agency unless that relationship is explicitly documented by jmpseat.

Do not imply that jmpseat content is official airline, airport, union, employer,
security, or operational guidance.

## Not An Operational Source

jmpseat is not an official operational source.

Do not use jmpseat as a substitute for official/employer systems, live
operations sources, security procedures, duty/rest guidance, schedules, dispatch
or crew tracking systems, safety guidance, legal advice, HR guidance, union
guidance, or company policy.

## Feedback

If you send feedback about jmpseat, jmpseat may use it to improve the product,
support, safety, policy, operations, and private-beta experience.

Do not include sensitive work details, passenger information, exact crew hotel
details, live operations details, confidential documents, or security-sensitive
information in feedback.
